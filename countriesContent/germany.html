Germany

IGermany is considered the third preferred destination for international study abroad aspirants, next to the United States and UK. Top-notch quality of education, scientifically oriented courses, nominal or zero tuition fee, and availability of numerous scholarships attract Indian students to pursue their higher education in Germany.

Ranked 6th on the UN Development Programme’s Human Development Index (HDI), 2014, Germany is the technological hub of Europe A degree from a German university is highly respected by employers worldwide, especially in the fields of science and engineering Approximately 83% of the Indian students opt for STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) courses (master’s and doctorate degrees) in German universities

Excellent funding opportunities, availability of well-equipped research facilities at universities, and independent research institutions, along with exceptional research opportunities in German companies make Germany a favourable location for students interested in obtaining a doctorate degree.

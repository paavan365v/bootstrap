About Us

Our core activity lies in assisting students to make the right choice with regard to pursuing education in overseas educational institutions. PRAGHH provides information regarding higher education in various countries such as UK, USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Ireland, France, Germany, Dubai, Switzerland, Malaysia and many more. PRAGHH, a pioneer in the field of 'GLOBAL EDUCATION' has brought the concept of overseas education to the doorstep of every student. Our excellent branch network and association with leading institutions worldwide and impeccable reputation helps us to deliver top quality education services. In addition to professional academic and career counseling, PRAGHH also offers various students quality services such as aptitude testing, course and university selection, streamlined application process guidance and assistance, admission assistance, visa guidance, accommodation assistance, test prep for IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GMAT, GRE, SAT and ACT. We also provide bank loan assistance, travel assistance, foreign exchange assistance, medical and travel insurance and comprehensive pre-departure guidance. Presently PRAGHH represents a wide portfolio of reputed international institutions & works closely with several organizations, high commissions & education bodies. PRAGHH is India's Leading Overseas Education Consultancy and has helped thousands of students achieve their dreams . Despite students/parents knowing what they wish to study and where, they usually avail our professional services to ensure a quality and hassle free application process thus allowing them to focus on their academics and job.
